Project / Maaike Girardin

Summary:

This project was initiated in Semester 2, where a client, named "Maaike Girardin" wanted us (a team of 6 media design students) to make a website for her. She is a musician with a burning passion for music with a feeling.

The main work done on this project was designing the website with her needs. We understood what she wanted, had multiple meetings with her and scribbled, drew, sketched and prototyped her ideal vision of her portfolio. We had multiple iterations, as we wanted to make the best out of her needs, wants and visions.

As this was our first project for a real client, we gave our hearts and souls to make it the best we could with the skills we had then. We had thousands of meetings, where we shared what we wanted the website to look like, shared ideas and our own visions. We created it with pure HTML, CSS and JS.

Visit: Maaike's Hosted Website Showcase

Challenges Faced:

How to manage our time in a big group.

Coding was still a big problem, as we just started out.

How to combine what we can do with the needs of the client, without sacrificing anything.

Technologies Used:

GIT for merging and collective collaboration with teammates from our group.

HTML, CSS, JS for the creation, designing and webpage behaviour.

Figma & Adobe XD for collaborative designing.
